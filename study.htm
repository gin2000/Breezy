5 studying tips:

  1. Plan ahead

    2. It's a bad idea to start studing last minutes. You get overwhealmed and tired. you start to forget everything you studied and you are simply discouraged. Try to retain information on daily Basis

  2. Pick the right place for you

    3. Some people like quiet places like libraries, others like study groups. Choose a place that makes inspired and comfortable

  3. Ask for help

    4. Whether it's a professor or a peer, talking to someone else will help you get unstuck and save time.

  4. Do things that help you concentrated

    5. Some people feel more focused listening to study music. If that's the case for have a study music list to save time

  5. Take breaks

    6. Don't study for hours without stopping. You're a human and your brain needs to rest.
